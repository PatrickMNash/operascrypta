Aught Cleaver

  • 25 November 2025
  • poetry

Suspended manifest banality,
dyadic acquitted maestros,
estranged
per lilliputians of allotted lacuna.

Surreptitious impetus,
allocated vicissitudes,
begets demented dynamistic concretions
amalgamating twain.
